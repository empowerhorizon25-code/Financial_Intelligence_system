## WHAT TO DO IF THINGS SLOW DOWN

**Twitter not getting replies?**
- Post more frequently (7 times/day instead of 4)
- Reply to trending stock tweets with your link
- Use hashtags: #stocks #trading #ai #fintech #signals

**Reddit getting deleted?**
- You pitched too hard. Delete, post again as pure value next week.
- Keep links in comments, not titles

**DMs not converting?**
- Switch to Script 3 (relationship building, not pitching)
- Ask "What's your biggest problem?" first
- Mention price only after they express interest

**No signups at all?**
- Test the link from phone (might be broken)
- Check if app is actually running
- Ask yourself: would YOU click this pitch?
